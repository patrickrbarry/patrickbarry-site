About

Background

I started in law — tech and media, San Francisco in the nineties — but the pull of building things was too strong. Two decades of operating followed: VP roles at Yahoo!, CMO at Demandforce through its acquisition by Intuit, COO at Logikcull. I was lucky enough to be coached by Bill Campbell during some of that time, and it changed how I think about leadership and teams permanently.

Now I run Greenhouse at Orrick Tech Studio, where I work with early-stage founders on the things that actually matter — team dynamics, decision-making under uncertainty, how to keep moving when you don't have answers. It's the best job I've ever had.

Outside of work: books, music, galleries, and wherever a trail leads.
